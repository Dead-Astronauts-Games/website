Dead Astronauts Secures €4M In Oversubscribed Seed Round

Former Massive Entertainment and Epic Games veterans are reinventing open worlds at Swedish-based game studio

Malmo, Sweden, 10 February, 2025 - Swedish game developer Dead Astronauts has raised €4 million in their first seed funding round led by Behold Ventures. Founded in 2024 by Tobias Nyman, Erik Morin, Robert Moschos, and Thomas Tedemalm, industry veterans from Massive Entertainment, Ubisoft, and Epic Games, Dead Astronauts is now expanding beyond its core team of four, by bringing together top-tier talent from both AAA and indie studios to fuel their development ambitions.

Seed round lead investor, Behold Ventures, is joined by co-investors Lifelike Capital, 1Up Ventures, Acequia Capital, and prominent angel investors Are Mack Growen, Matt Bilbey, Klaas Kersting, Asbjoern Malte Soendergaard, and Kristian Segerstrale.

Dead Astronauts is developing an open world action-adventure game codenamed VEIL. Built in Unreal Engine 5, VEIL harnesses advanced procedural content generation and custom tools to craft a mysterious world inspired by the vast Nordic wilderness, and the unexplored cosmos.
